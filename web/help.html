How can we help you grow?

Find answers to common questions or reach out to our botanical experts.

How does the Spaced Repetition System work?

Seedling calculates the optimal time to review words based on your previous performance. Words you find easy transition to "Stable" status and appear less frequently, while difficult words stay in "Nurturing" and pop up more often.

Is Seedling free to use?

The core Seedling experience is entirely free. You can plant, nurture, and master unlimited words. Our "Greenhouse" subscription offers advanced analytics, unlimited offline voice synthesis, and custom learning themes.

Can I sync my data between devices?

Yes! If you sign in, your entire garden is securely synced to our cloud forest. Plant on your phone during your commute, and harvest on your tablet at home.

How do I report a bug or translation error?

We're human (and mascots)! If you find an error, please click the contact button below or email us directly with a screenshot of the issue.
