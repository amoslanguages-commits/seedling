The Method

How your Seedling
Garden grows

01
🌱

Plant Your Seeds

Choose a language and topic. Seedling plants 3 foundation words for new learners — your starter garden. Each word is presented with pronunciation, definition, and an example sentence.

02
💧

Nourish Daily

Return each day for a 5-minute session. The engine serves exactly the right words at the right time — mixing SRS reviews with one fresh new word to keep growth sustainable.

03
🌳

Watch Mastery Bloom

Words evolve through 5 mastery levels: Seedling → Sprout → Sapling → Tree → Ancient Oak. Each level requires deeper recall challenges. Real knowledge, earned naturally.

04
🏆

Harvest & Share

Export vocabulary sets, challenge friends in live duels, and showcase your growing Garden to the world. Your garden is your proof of mastery.
