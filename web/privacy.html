Privacy Policy

At Amos languages, we believe that your learning journey should be private and secure. This Privacy Policy outlines how we handle your information when you use the Seedling application and website.

1. Information We Collect

In order to provide a premium language learning experience, we collect the following types of information:

  • Account Information: If you create an account, we store your name, email address, and authentication tokens provided by our secure login partners.
  • Learning Progress: We track your vocabulary progress, including which words you've planted, your quiz accuracy, and your streak data. This is used to drive our Spaced Repetition System (SRS).
  • Usage Data: We collect anonymous technical information such as device type, OS version, and app performance logs to help us squash bugs and improve the experience.

2. How We Use Data

Your data is used exclusively to:

  • Synchronize your learning garden across multiple devices.
  • Optimize the difficulty and timing of your review sessions.
  • Provide technical support and respond to your inquiries.
  • Send essential service updates (e.g., account recovery).

3. Data Protection

We use industry-standard encryption and security protocols to protect your data. We do not sell your personal information to third parties. Your learning data is stored securely using Supabase's encrypted database infrastructure.

4. Your Rights

You have the right to access, export, or delete your personal data at any time. If you wish to permanently delete your account and all associated learning progress, please contact us at the email address below.
